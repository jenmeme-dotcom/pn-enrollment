## 🎨 CUSTOMIZATION SHORTCUTS

### Change Institution Name
1. Open `index.html` with Notepad
2. Ctrl+H (Find & Replace)
3. Find: "Broward-Miami Health Institute"
4. Replace with: Your school name
5. Save

### Change Program Cost
1. Open `app.js` with Notepad
2. Find: `baseAmount = 12500`
3. Change to: `baseAmount = 15000` (or your cost)
4. Save
5. Refresh browser

### Change Colors
1. Open `styles.css` with Notepad
2. Find: `:root {` (first 20 lines)
3. Change:
   - `--primary-color: #0066cc;` (blue)
   - `--secondary-color: #00a86b;` (green)
4. Save
5. Refresh browser

### Add Your Video
1. Save your video as: `enrollment-process.mp4`
2. Put in same folder as `index.html`
3. Refresh browser
4. Done!

### Update Contact Info
In Step 5 (Milestones), find and update:
- Student Services phone
- Program Director email
- Billing phone
